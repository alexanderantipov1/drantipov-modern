# Dr. Alexander Antipov - Board-Certified Oral Surgeon

> Board-certified oral and maxillofacial surgeon specializing in dental implants, full-arch restoration, corrective jaw surgery, and facial cosmetic procedures in Roseville, California.

## About Dr. Alexander Antipov

Dr. Alexander V. Antipov is the founder of Fusion Dental Implants and a board-certified oral and maxillofacial surgeon based in Roseville, California. A graduate of Moscow State Medical Stomatology University and Loma Linda University School of Dentistry, he completed advanced training in oral and maxillofacial surgery at Albert Einstein College of Medicine.

Dr. Antipov is a Diplomate of the American Board of Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery, a member of the American Association of Oral & Maxillofacial Surgeons (AAOMS), and a member of the American Dental Association (ADA).

## Services & Expertise

### Full-Arch Implant Solutions
All-on-X restoration with same-day teeth using advanced techniques. Comprehensive full-arch dental implant solutions for patients missing most or all teeth.

### Single Tooth & Bridge Replacement
Precision implant placement for natural-looking results. Individual dental implant placement to replace single missing teeth or support dental bridges.

### Bone Grafting & Augmentation
Advanced techniques to restore bone volume for implant success. Bone grafting procedures to rebuild jawbone structure for patients who have experienced bone loss.

### Corrective Jaw Surgery (Orthognathic Surgery)
Orthognathic surgery to correct bite alignment and facial structure. Surgical correction of jaw misalignment, overbite, underbite, and facial asymmetry.

### Sleep Apnea Surgical Solutions
Surgical interventions for obstructive sleep apnea. Advanced surgical treatments for patients who haven't responded to CPAP or other non-surgical approaches.

### Scarless Mole Removal
Advanced radio wave surgery for bloodless, scar-free mole removal from the nose and face. Minimally invasive cosmetic procedures with excellent aesthetic outcomes.
